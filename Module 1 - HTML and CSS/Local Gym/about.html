Hers His
Norma was a personal trainer for years. Unfortunately, she was always too busy to date (or so she thought). She tried speed dating with some friends who convinced her and found it too awkward. She decided to treat it like another workout and trained her social skills like a muscle. That's when she met Mario. Mario grew up training in various forms of martial arts. Confident as he was when sparring, he was never quite able to bring that confidence into his social life. He dated on and off for years, but couldn't bring himself to feel like he could settle down. Well, unless he went to a local speed dating event and saw a personal trainer that worked at the gym he requented (spoiler: it was Norma!)
Theirs
Mario and Norma will be the first to tell you their first time talking wasn't like the movies. They were nervous, quiet, and didn't know how to start or hold a conversation. They decided to go to the gym together and that's when everything clicked. After what seemed like no time, they decided to become life-long workout partners. That's when Norma had the crazy idea to open their own gym and teach others what they figured out. Ten years later of sparring, speaking, training, and teaching, the idea doesn't seem so crazy anymore.
