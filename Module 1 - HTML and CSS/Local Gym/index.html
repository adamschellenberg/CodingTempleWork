"If love is free, why is it hard for people to find it?"
Train your body and train your heart

Tae-Kwon-Dating is serious about teaching you to defend your body and open your heart. Come work out with our physical trainers and martial arts professionals during the day, and meet with other single members of the gym during sponsored events! You'll be surprised just how confident our training will make you in social gatherings!
