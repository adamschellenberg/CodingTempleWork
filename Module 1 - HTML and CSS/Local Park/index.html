Where nature blooms

Come to the Johto National Park and be a part of nature, right from your backyard! Located conveniently between Routes 35 and 36, this beautiful nature reserve is always just a short walk or bike ride away.

Whether you come for a calming walk, relaxing bike ride, or our famous Bug-Catching Contest, you're sure to enjoy the beauty and wildlife that abounds in Goldenrod City's very own Johto National Park.

Bug-Catching Contest

Be a part of our world-famous Bug-Catching Contest, held every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday!

Compete against other trainers to catch the best bug Pokemon you can find.* But be quick, you've only got twenty minutes! Aim for #1 and receive the coveted Sun Stone, loved by certain grass Pokemon.

* Twenty Park Balls will be provided to each participant

Countless wonders for you to experience

  • Over 30 Pokemon to find and catch
  • Multiple walking and biking paths
  • Iconic wishing fountain
  • Dozens of picnic areas

But be careful trainers, if your eyes meet with another trainer, you're in for a challenge!
